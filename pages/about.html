Airnote

Who i am

I am Kenny Appiah, a first year student at the University of Technology, Mauritius (UTM) and the creator of AirNote, a website built by a harmonica player, for harmonica players. Born from a deep passion for the instrument, AirNote is a dedicated space where learners, improvisers, and music lovers come together to explore, practice, and grow one breath at a time.

Method

Youtube videos

I group the best videos to learn harmonica

Tablature

I use tablature to indicate the notes of a melody

Mission

Today it is difficult to find a harmonica teacher as most people is interested in playing guitare and piano. So this website is here to help those who want to try harmonica and ease their journey
